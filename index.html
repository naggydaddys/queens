ABOUT

Held yearly in Flushing Meadows Corona Park, queens, ny, the queens music festival is an iconic 4 day event that brings together the most renowned female preformers in the music industry.

More than a concert, more than 4 days, queens Music Festival has provided over 3 billion canned goods since it's inception. Distributing them to homeless shelters and domestic centers throughout new york; feeding families in need for months. For years, the concert has required canned goods in addition to the price of admission, and it always will.
